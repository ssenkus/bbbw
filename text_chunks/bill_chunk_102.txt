(d) Denial of credit for certain wind and solar leasing arrangements.—Section 45Y is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection: “(h) Denial of credit for wind and solar leasing arrangements.—No credit shall be determined under this section with respect to any production of electricity during the taxable year with respect to property described in paragraph (1) or (4) of section 25D(d) (as applied by substituting ‘lessee’ for ‘taxpayer’) if the taxpayer rents or leases such property to a third party during such taxable year.”.
