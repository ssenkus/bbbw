institution, and “(B) unless such organization is controlled by such institution or is described in section 509(a)(3) with respect to such institution for the taxable year, assets and net investment ‘related organization’ means, with respect to an educational institution, any organization which— “(A) controls, or is controlled by, such institution, “(B) is controlled by 1 or more persons which institution. “(h) Regulations.—The Secretary shall prescribe such regulations or other guidance as may be necessary to prevent avoidance of the tax under this section, including regulations or other tax imposed by this section.”.
